# AI Creative Automation Roadmap (2024-2025)

## Phase 1: Foundation (The Engine) - Q3 2024
- [ ] **Omni-Parser Development:** AI engine capable of extracting key moments from long-form video/audio.
- [ ] **Cross-Platform Template Engine:** Dynamic layout system for automatic aspect ratio adaptation (9:16, 1:1, 16:9).
- [ ] **MVP Launch:** Focus on "Video to Short-form" automation.

## Phase 2: Intelligence (The Brain) - Q4 2024
- [ ] **Semantic Context Awareness:** AI that writes captions and hashtags based on visual/audio context.
- [ ] **Style Transfer API:** Apply brand-specific color grading and typography automatically.
- [ ] **A/B Variation Engine:** Generate 5 variations of the same content for performance testing.

## Phase 3: Ecosystem (The Network) - Q1 2025
- [ ] **Direct Publishing Integration:** One-click deployment to YT Shorts, TikTok, IG, and LinkedIn.
- [ ] **Feedback Loop:** AI learns from engagement metrics to optimize future creative edits.
- [ ] **Enterprise API:** Allowing creative agencies to build custom workflows on our infrastructure.

## Phase 4: Expansion (The Standard) - Q2 2025
- [ ] **Multi-Modal Mastery:** Expanding from video/audio to 3D and Interactive content.
- [ ] **Global Localization:** Real-time AI dubbing and cultural adaptation for global campaigns.
